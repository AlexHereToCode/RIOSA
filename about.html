I believe that one of my key strengths is being able to be optimistic despite all uncertainties life has and will give to me as i am fond into taking up challenges while accepting both success and failure in a balanced way to move forward. I became passionate to this field of work last year when i discovered the world of programming. Therefore, I always challenge myself to improve my progress and steadily grow to attain these goals.
